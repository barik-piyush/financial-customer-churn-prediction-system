A powerful prediction of churn

Financial customer churn prediction is the process of using machine learning algorithms and data analytics to identify customers at high risk of closing their accounts, reducing their product usage, or switching to a competitor. By analyzing historical data‚Äîsuch as transaction history, demographics information, service interactions, and product usage patterns‚Äîfinancial institutions can pinpoint behavioral indicators of disengagement or dissatisfaction.
